List Pal

This bot speaks a line in your list aloud and then waits for you to say "next" or "okay" before speaking the next line. Begin by pressing "Start talk" button.

Google Chrome 33 or greater is required. Also ensure both your microfone and speakers are working.

next, okay - say the next line
again, repeat - say the line again
back - say the previous line
